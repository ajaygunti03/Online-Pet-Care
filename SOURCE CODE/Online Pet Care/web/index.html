ABSTRACT

Attachment, a normal behavior among social animals, is quite significant since owners worry about their pets and take care of them because of this affective connection. There are not enough research studies that focus on attachment between owners and their pets. The general objective of this study was to identify attachment behaviors, directed toward their owners, in pets of different types, age groups, and sexes in an experimental situation.
